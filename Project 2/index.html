That's What
I like

High-quality flour should be dry, without lumps, crumble when squeezed in the palm of your hand. The color of the flour depends on the variety. It is advisable to choose coarse flour, dark in color, not white. Flour should not have an extraneous odor, especially the smell of mold. The flour should taste sweet, not bitter, not sour. There should be no crunching on the teeth from flour.
